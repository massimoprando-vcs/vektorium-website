Frequently Asked Questions

What is cyber threat intelligence (CTI)?

Cyber threat intelligence is the collection, analysis, and dissemination of information about current and potential cyber threats. It helps organizations understand threat actors, their motivations, capabilities, and attack patterns to improve security posture.

How does Cypher Sentinel operationalize threat intelligence?

Cypher Sentinel automatically ingests threat intelligence feeds and correlates IOCs across all 72 security engines — SIEM, EDR, firewall, and network monitoring — enabling real-time detection and automated response without manual IOC management.

What are Indicators of Compromise (IOCs)?

IOCs are artifacts or evidence that indicate a potential security breach — including malicious IP addresses, domain names, file hashes, email addresses, and behavioral patterns. Cypher Sentinel ingests and correlates millions of IOCs in real-time.

Does Cypher Sentinel support STIX/TAXII standards?

Yes. Cypher Sentinel fully supports STIX (Structured Threat Information eXpression) and TAXII (Trusted Automated eXchange of Intelligence Information) standards for threat intelligence sharing and ingestion.

How does dark web monitoring work?

Cypher Sentinel monitors dark web forums, marketplaces, and paste sites for mentions of your organization, leaked credentials, data breach notifications, and threat actor communications targeting your industry.
